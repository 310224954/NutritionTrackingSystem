Calories tables

Welcome!

This aplication is intended to calculate your daily calories consumption.

Also you will be able to compose your diet plan with the help of application.

Let's start maybe with short introduction what you will find in each tab of navigation bar on top.

Food tab is the heart of the application inside you can find such sub-tabs as product-tables. Where you can check nutrition values and create your own products based on ingredient db, which later can be added to your diet plan.

As you can see on above picture you can add new products to the table. But remember to not duplicate existing positions!

Based on the existing product table you can also create the whole dished and calculate the approximate amount of calories and all the other nutritional values of it.

Another tab witch which you can expand your knowledge about diet is articles tab.

Reading articles on topics that interest you, is not only thing you can do with them. You can also add new articles, just don't forget aboutto tag the product tags they concerns!

Finally Profile tab.

Place where you can check all prods that you have chosen as ingredients of your diet. You can update quantity added to your diet remember after clicking add button in products table, product is added with suggested weight.
